## SUGGESTED ICON SOURCES:
1. **Lucide React** (Already installed) - https://lucide.dev/
   - Clean, consistent, open source
   - Perfect for professional dashboards
   
2. **Heroicons** - https://heroicons.com/
   - By Tailwind team, matches your UI library
   
3. **Tabler Icons** - https://tabler-icons.io/
   - 4000+ icons, MIT license
   
4. **Remix Icon** - https://remixicon.com/
   - Neutral style, perfect for business apps
   
5. **Phosphor Icons** - https://phosphoricons.com/
   - Flexible, modern design system

6. **React Icons** - https://react-icons.github.io/react-icons/
   - Collection of popular icon sets in one package
